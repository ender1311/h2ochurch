# Project Overview

## What this is

The web application for H2O Church in Columbus, Ohio.

## Audience

- **Newcomers** looking for service times, location, and what to expect.
- **Regular attenders** wanting messages, events, and group information.
- **Members** who want to serve, give, and stay connected.

## Planned sections

- **Home** — welcome and next steps.
- **Visit** — service times, location, parking, and FAQs.
- **Messages** — recent sermons to watch or listen to.
- **Groups** — find and join a community group.
- **Events** — upcoming church events.
- **Give** — online giving.
